American Journal of Public Health:
Title: Retail tobacco outlet density and youth cigarette smoking: A propensity-modeling approach.
Authors: SP Novak, SF Reardon, SW Raudenbush, SL Buka
Source: AJPH 96: 670-676, Apr 2006.

to check balance, calculated within stratum correlation of X and T. "When we found a significant 
correlation in any stratum for any X..."

Title: Relationship between drug abuse and intimate partner violence: A longitudinal study among women 
receiving methadone
Authors: N El-Bassel, L Gilbert, E Wu, H Go, and J Hill
Source: AJPH 95: 465-470, Mar 2005.

robust tests such as Kolmogorov-Smirnov and Shapiro-Wilk

Title: A community-level HIV prevention intervention for inner-city women: results of the women and 
infants demonstration projects.
Authors: JL Lauby, PJ Smith, M Stark, B Person, J Adams
Source: AJPH 90: 216-222, Feb 2000.

t-tests (stat. sig. of diff in change scores)
other issues--community-level intervention (t communities and matched c communities)--only 7 communities, 
but do analysis at person-level

Other sources;
Title: Using propensity scores to help design observational studies: Application to the tobacco 
litigation
Author: Donald B Rubin
Source: Health Services & Outcomes Research Methodology 2: 169-188, 2001.

Good diagnostics described:
1) standardized difference in means of pscore (though we would advocate all Xs)
2) ratio of variances of the pscore in T and C groups
3) for each covariate, the ratio of the variance of the residuals orthogonal to the pscore for T group to 
that for C group

Title: Firearm violence exposure and serious violent behavior
Authors: JB Bingenheimer, RT Brennan, FJ Earls
Source: Science 308: 1323-1326, May 27, 2005.

hypothesis tests before and after matching
(not a bad paper otherwise--subclassification, and discard top and bottom deciles because of poor 
overlap)
